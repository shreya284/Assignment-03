About Us

Founded in 2010 by H. H. Shri Vishwavallabha Theertha Swamiji of Shri Sode Vadiraja Mutt – one of the 700 plus years old Ashta Mutts associated with the world famous Shri Krishna temple of Udupi in Karnataka – Shri MadhwaVadiraja Institute of Technology & Management (SMVITM), situated at Bantakal in Udupi has carved a niche for itself in imparting quality engineering education in the coastal Karnataka region. Shri Swamiji started the institute with the sublime objective of providing quality higher education to the needy student community at affordable costs. The motto Engineering your Career and Character with Care well reflects the objectives and philosophy of the institute. Despite, being the youngest institute in the region, SMVITM has made remarkable strides in its journey towards excellence over the past five years and has emerged as the most promising engineering institute with its well-qualified and motivated faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure and distinguished learning faci lities. At present, SMVITM, with a total student intake of 441 per year, offers Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) courses in Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering Electronics & Communication Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Currently, 1562 students are pursuing their engineering education at the institute. Despite being located in a rural area, SMVITM ensures that its students are well trained in existing engineering practices and acquainted with the latest industrial trends.

Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

Computer Science and Engineering(CSE) Department provides the best quality of computer education. It teaches the students about the various types of trending technologies such as AI,ML,CN,DBMS,Networking ,Data Structures and Algorithms etc.

Electronics and Communication(E&C) Engineering

Electronics and Communication(E&C) Department provides the utmost knowledge and informational teaching to the students on Digital and Analog Electronics, Signal Communication, Image Processing, Micro-controllers etc.It also impacts on the base knowledge of Electronics and it's various domains present in it.

Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering(CVE) Department gives the good quality and better foundation of knowledge to the students on Construction and Management,Fluid Mechanics, Geotechnical Engineering,Environmental Engineering, Design of Steel and RC-Structures and many more etc, especially Survey Practice to give a practical impact about the outdoor exploration and discovery of work environment in the outside world to all.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical(ME) Engineering Department provides the feasible and suitable quality of impact knowledge to the students on the various domain fields present in it such as:Energy and Environment, Heat Transfer, CAD Design ,Elements of Mechanical Engineering etc in order to acknowledge and present the knowledge of Mechanical core fields to the students and overall.
